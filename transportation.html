Transportation

This page explains how visitors can travel to and around Taniti. It also includes rough price ranges to support budgeting and trip planning.

Getting to Taniti

By air

Flights arrive at the main airport. Most visitors use a shuttle, taxi, or rental car to get to their lodging.

By sea

Some trips include ferry service or small cruises that dock near the harbor.

Getting around the island

Common options

Visitors typically use taxis, shuttles, bike rentals, or walking for short distances. Car rentals are helpful for day trips and viewpoints.

Accessibility

Many visitor areas are walkable. For longer trips, a taxi or shuttle is usually the easiest option.

Estimated price ranges

These are rough estimates to help with planning. Actual costs can change based on distance, time of day, and season.

Transportation option Typical use Estimated range
Airport taxi Airport to downtown or beach area $15 to $30
Airport shuttle Shared rides to lodging areas $8 to $18
Local bus Budget travel between main areas $2 to $5 per ride
Bike rental Short trips and sightseeing $10 to $20 per day
Car rental Day trips and flexible schedules $45 to $80 per day

Tip: If your lodging is near dining and attractions, you can often spend less on transportation.
