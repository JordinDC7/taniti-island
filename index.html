General island information

Location and climate

Taniti is a small tropical island with warm weather most of the year. Expect humid days and cooler evenings near the coast.

Language and currency

English is commonly used in visitor areas. Most businesses accept major credit cards, and cash is helpful for small purchases.

Safety and services

Taniti is considered a safe destination. Visitors should still follow standard travel precautions and keep valuables secure.
