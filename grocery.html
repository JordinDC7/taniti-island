Grocery and markets

This page lists common shopping options for visitors who want quick groceries, snacks, or local items.

Shopping options

Local markets

Fresh produce, simple meals, and local crafts. The night market is popular for quick bites.

Small grocery stores

Basic groceries, water, and snacks. These are usually located near downtown and lodging areas.

Pharmacy and essentials

Personal care items and basic travel essentials are available at several shops near the visitor center.

For dining options with locations and photos, visit the Dining page.
