Lodging and accommodations

Here are a few common places to stay on Taniti. I added pictures and quick notes so it is easier to compare options.

Lodging styles

Budget stays

Small hotels and guesthouses that focus on affordability. Good for short stays and flexible schedules.

Typical features: basic rooms, easy check in, simple amenities.

Family friendly

Mid range hotels and condo style rentals with space for groups and kid friendly features.

Typical features: larger rooms, nearby dining, walkable areas.

Luxury resorts

Higher end resorts with on site services and activities. Great for relaxing trips and special occasions.

Typical features: pool access, concierge services, spa options.

Eco and nature stays

Cabins and small lodges near scenic areas. A good fit for visitors focused on hiking and outdoor activities.

Typical features: quiet locations, guided tours, local experiences.

Travel style examples

Backpackers

Budget stays or small guesthouses, plus easy access to transit and markets.

Couples

Resorts and beachfront stays that keep things simple and close to dining.

Families

Family friendly hotels or rentals with space and walkable food options.

Outdoor trips

Eco and nature stays closer to trails, viewpoints, and guided activities.

These are examples, not official listings. Availability and prices change by season.

How to choose

Pick your travel style

If your main goal is exploring, budget and eco stays are a strong match. If your goal is convenience, family friendly or resort options can reduce travel time.

Check proximity

Choose lodging near the beach or downtown depending on what you plan to do most. Transportation costs can also change based on distance.

See rough transportation price ranges on the Transportation page.

Plan meals

If you prefer walking to meals, consider locations near dining areas. Restaurant locations are listed on the Dining page.
