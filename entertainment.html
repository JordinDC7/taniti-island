Attractions and things to do

This page combines entertainment and sightseeing so visitors can find activities in one place. Photos are included to make options easier to recognize and compare.

If you previously used a separate sightseeing page, it is now grouped here for easier browsing. You can still access the older page at Sightseeing.

Featured attractions

Local History Museum

Learn about Taniti's history, culture, and local traditions. Good for families and rainy day plans.

Location: Old Town near the harbor

Beaches and swimming

Relax on the shoreline, swim in calm waters, and enjoy sunset walks along the coast.

Guided hikes

Explore scenic trails with local guides. Options range from easy walks to longer hikes.

Markets and local crafts

Browse handmade items and local goods. Great for souvenirs and quick snacks.

See more shopping details on the Grocery and Markets page.

Sightseeing highlights

Waterfall viewpoint

A short trip outside town with a marked overlook. Morning visits are usually less crowded.

Boat tours

Coastal tours run throughout the day. Some tours include snorkeling stops.

Historic sites

Visit preserved locations and guided exhibits that highlight island history and architecture.
